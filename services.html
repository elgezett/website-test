All services and capabilities

Comprehensive manufacturing solutions from design to delivery

It starts with an idea

Every part goes through a complete in-house process chain: from 3D design and model construction to aluminum casting, machining, and final inspection. The result: components ready for immediate use — durable, precise, and made in Berlin.

Construction

Our specialists optimize your 3D CAD data for the entire manufacturing process — from prototype to series production. With modern CAD/CAM systems and simulation tools, we detect and eliminate potential errors early, ensuring efficiency and precision at every stage.

Model & Tooling

We produce precise models and molds for our aluminum foundry using advanced 3D milling and rapid prototyping. This enables complex geometries and fast turnaround times — tailored to your requirements.

Foundry

Our in-house foundry combines short paths, flexibility, and decades of experience. We offer sand and permanent mold casting for prototypes, small batches, and serial production — powered by sustainable, electric melting systems and 3D-printed cores for complex contours.

Machining

Our CNC machining centers enable precise turning and milling — from single parts to serial runs. With 5-axis technology, automation systems, and continuous process optimization, we guarantee maximum flexibility, short lead times, and consistent quality.

Serial Production

From small batches to thousands of parts — our series production adapts to your needs. Complete in-house process control ensures reliable delivery, flexibility, and consistent precision even under tight timelines.

Quality Assurance

Quality is more than accuracy — it's our promise. Certified to ISO 9001, we apply tactile, scanning, and optical measurement technologies to ensure every part meets your specifications. From first-sample reports to 100% inspections, we deliver reliability that's measurable.

Packaging

We make sure your parts arrive safely and sustainably. Our packaging solutions are tailored to each component — from reusable systems to custom inserts — reducing waste and ensuring optimal protection in transit.

Assembly & Additional Services

Beyond manufacturing, we handle assembly, functional testing, surface finishing, and heat treatment. This complete service ensures your components are ready for direct integration — tested, refined, and delivered from one source.
