All Services & Capabilities

Complete Manufacturing Solutions

From initial concept to final delivery, Werner Lichy provides comprehensive manufacturing services that cover every aspect of the production process. Our integrated approach ensures seamless transitions between different manufacturing stages, reducing lead times and maintaining consistent quality throughout.

Core Services

3D Design & CAD

Advanced CAD/CAM systems for precise design optimization and simulation.

Model Construction

High-precision models and molds using advanced 3D milling and rapid prototyping.

Aluminum Casting

In-house foundry with sand and permanent mold casting capabilities.

CNC Machining

5-axis technology with automation systems for maximum precision and efficiency.

Quality Assurance

ISO 9001 certified with tactile, scanning, and optical measurement technologies.

Assembly & Testing

Complete assembly services with functional testing and surface finishing.

Specialized Capabilities

Our expertise extends beyond standard manufacturing to include specialized services such as heat treatment, surface finishing, packaging solutions, and comprehensive quality control. We work with a wide range of materials and can accommodate both prototype development and serial production requirements.

Industry Applications

We serve diverse industries including automotive, aerospace, medical devices, and industrial machinery. Our flexible manufacturing approach allows us to adapt to specific industry requirements while maintaining the highest standards of precision and reliability.

Komplette Fertigungslösungen

Von der ersten Idee bis zur finalen Lieferung bietet Werner Lichy umfassende Fertigungsdienstleistungen, die jeden Aspekt des Produktionsprozesses abdecken. Unser integrierter Ansatz gewährleistet nahtlose Übergänge zwischen verschiedenen Fertigungsstufen und reduziert Durchlaufzeiten bei gleichbleibender Qualität.
